One Day Album Cover

For the cover of One Day by Doan, I wanted to visually capture the emotional contrast between struggle and hope that runs through the song. The dark, textured background reflects life's challenges and the weight of time, while the magenta triangle adds a sense of urgency and change—representing the breakthrough moments we all wait for. The distressed edges of the triangle emphasize the raw, imperfect nature of this journey, echoing the song’s themes of resilience.

The bold "ONE DAY" in white typography was chosen to stand strong against the backdrop, symbolizing a beacon of hope and the promise that the right moment will come, even after a long wait. The simplicity of the typeface, set against the textured background, reflects the idea of holding on to hope despite the chaos. This design brings together the themes of anticipation, struggle, and the eventual breakthrough that is at the heart of Doan’s song.
