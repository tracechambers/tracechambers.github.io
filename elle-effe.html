Elle Effe Restaurant Poster

This poster for Elle Effe Italian Restaurant is designed to instantly transport viewers to an authentic Italian dining experience. The main focus is a beautifully plated seafood pasta, paired with a glass of white wine, set against the warm ambiance of the restaurant’s interior. The goal is to highlight the fresh, high-quality ingredients and cozy atmosphere, inviting viewers to imagine themselves enjoying a meal at Elle Effe.

For typography, I chose an elegant serif font for "Elle Effe" to convey sophistication, paired with a clean sans-serif for "Italian Restaurant" and the address below, keeping the design approachable and clear. The soft border frames the composition, drawing attention to the inviting scene and reinforcing Elle Effe’s identity as a charming, traditional Italian eatery.
