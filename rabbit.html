Rabbit

This logo was created for an upscale boutique hotel company. The client requested a word-mark logo that prominently features the color red. My intent was to craft a design that embodies luxury and elegance while maintaining a welcoming and approachable feel.

The choice of red was deliberate, symbolizing warmth, passion, and sophistication. The typography is sleek and modern, with clean lines that convey a sense of refinement and exclusivity. The subtle serif details add a touch of classic elegance, ensuring the logo stands out as both timeless and contemporary.

To enhance the welcoming aspect, I carefully balanced the intensity of the red with a harmonious layout, ensuring that the logo feels inviting and not overpowering.
