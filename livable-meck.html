Livable Meck

This logo was created for Livable Meck, a community-focused initiative aimed at enhancing the quality of life in Mecklenburg County. The client wanted a logo that conveys growth, sustainability, and community. My goal was to develop a design that feels both nurturing and progressive.

The logo features a harmonious blend of teal and sage green, colors that represent growth, tranquility, and a connection to nature. The abstract leaf and bird elements symbolize a commitment to environmental sustainability and a thriving, vibrant community.

The typography is clean and modern, with "LIVABLE" in a lighter weight to emphasize the nurturing aspect, while "MECK" is bold to convey strength and stability. The overall design aims to create a sense of balance and unity, reflecting the collaborative efforts of the community to create a livable and sustainable future.

The simplicity of the design ensures versatility and adaptability across various mediums, from digital platforms to printed materials, making it an effective and memorable representation of Livable Meck's mission and values.
