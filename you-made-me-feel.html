You Made Me Feel Cover Image

For the cover of You Made Me Feel by Blankmanship, I wanted to reflect the intense, emotional contrast at the heart of the song. The stormy sky, blending lavender, purple, and gray, sets a moody, somewhat nostalgic tone, while the hazy clouds evoke a sense of uncertainty. The vibrant lightning bolt cutting through the sky represents the sudden, electrifying moments that stir deep feelings, creating a visual parallel to the emotional impact of the song.

The soft, white typography of "You Made Me Feel" was placed at the top to give a sense of light piercing through the darker elements below. The balance of light and dark, with the silhouetted trees grounding the scene, captures the song’s themes of vulnerability and overwhelming emotions. The design evokes both the calm before the storm and the powerful rush of feeling that comes with it.
