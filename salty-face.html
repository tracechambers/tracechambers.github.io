Salty Face

This logo was created for an ocean-themed street-wear company. Drawing inspiration from the dynamic and ever-changing nature of the ocean, I employed a color palette rich in blues and turquoises to evoke a strong coastal vibe. The bold and modern typography was chosen to ensure the brand name stands out, making a memorable impression at first glance.

I incorporated elements that suggest movement and energy, to go with the essence of the brand. The logo’s versatility will allow a high-quality appearance across various applications, from apparel to marketing materials.

Through this design, I aimed to resonate with the brand’s core values and target audience, effectively communicating a lifestyle centered around the adventure and coastal living. This project exemplifies my ability to create impactful and contextually relevant branding solutions that enhance a company’s identity.
